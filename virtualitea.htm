Just follow these simple steps;

  1. Purchase your VirtualiTEA© Starter Kit.
  2. Log—in to your free account and select all the flavors your taste buds are craving.
  3. Plug in your reusable VirtualiTEA© bag to start infusing.
  4. Once your customized tea bag is fully infused, steep in boiling water for 5 minutes.
  5. After enjoying your personalized tea experience, plug in your VirtualiTEA© bag to clear previous tastes.
  6. Repeat for a new VirtualiTEA© experience!
