Pumpkin Pie Recipes

MMmmmmmm....Pumpkin Pie...with pistachios?!

Filling

  • 1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup crushed pistachios

Pastry

  • 4.5 oz plain flour
  • pinch of salt
  • 2.5oz hard margarine or butter
  • 3 tsp cold water

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 425F
  • Sieve the flour and salt into a bowl
  • Add the margarine or butter and mix until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs
  • Add the cold water to the flour mixture. Using your hand to firm up the mixture to good rolling consistency
  • Roll out pastry and place in pie plate
  • Whisk pumpkin, pistachios, sweetened condensed milk, eggs, spices and salt in medium bowl until smooth. Pour into crust
  • Bake 15 minutes, then reduce oven temperature to 350F and continue baking 35 to 40 minutes
