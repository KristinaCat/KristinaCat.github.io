Your Better Self Awaits At The Roost

Life can sure keep you busy. Balancing a full time job, children, and all the other adult responsibilities, it can be exhausting! Everyone needs a break. A place to go to get away from it all and recharge to survive another day of your hectic life. Don't you wish you could get back to the core basics of sanity and leave the hustle and bustle behind for a while?

Welcome to your getaway. We have plenty of vacation packages available to choose from, so no matter what your idea of relaxation is we can accommodate. From outdoors activities such as horseback riding to a game of giant Jenga, or stay indoors and enjoy your in-suite private jacuzzi followed by a glass of wine in front of your personal fireplace. The choice is yours.

The Roost is located 5 miles from the town of Timbucktoo on 247 acres of varying terrain. Containing 2 ponds, plenty of wildlife and nature to adore with 4 separate buildings with a total of 20 rooms to choose from, and plenty of seasonal activities that will make each visit unique. Here at The Roost, we are eager to accommodate any special requests you have to create a truly personalized getaway to remember. Check out our full list of amenities and room options to start planning your perfect stay at The Roost!
