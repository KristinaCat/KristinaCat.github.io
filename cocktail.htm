Summer

My favorite summer past-time is relaxing on the patio with friends. Add a cocktail or two and you have a perfect afternoon! A cool refreshing drink with friends truly is the best way to beat the heat. So as you can imagine, our patio sure has seen plenty of different drink concoctions over the years. But after all that not so pain staking research, we have found the winning summertime beverage: Lemonade!

Not only because they pair well with lemonade, but by choosing fruit that is harvested in summer you can be sure your drinks will be fresh and plentiful. These cocktails can be prepped in advance so there is minimal work to do when the heat hits. Whether you prefer blueberries, strawberries, or raspberries, here are some cocktail recipes that I am sure will please everyone at your next summer gathering.

See you on the patio!

Blueberry Vodka Lemonade

Ingredients

  • 14 ounces of Vodka
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 2 cups lemonade
  • 1 ¾ cups carbonated water
  • 2 cups ice

Instructions

  1. Wash the blueberries and place in a 1 quart mason jar
  2. Fill the mason jar with the Vodka
  3. Tightly close the mason jar and let sit for 24 hours
  4. After 24 hours, strain the blueberries from the Vodka and store them in the freezer
  5. Blend 2 cups of lemonade and 2 cups of ice in a blender until slushy
  6. In an 8 ounce glass, mix 1 ounce of the flavored Vodka and 2 tablespoons of the carbonated water
  7. Add the lemonade slushy and a few of the frozen blueberries to the glass
  8. Repeat above steps as desired

Strawberry Rum Lemonade

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberry flavored Rum
  • 2 cups fresh strawberries
  • 1 cup lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon grated lemon peel
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 cup sugar

Instructions

  1. Bring 2 cups of the water and the sugar to a boil, reduce heat and simmer until sugar has dissolved
  2. Stir in the lemon juice and grated peel, then let cool
  3. Once the mixture has cooled completely, strain into a pitcher
  4. In a blender, puree the cleaned strawberries then add to the pitcher
  5. Stir thoroughly
  6. Add the rum and remaining water
  7. Stir thoroughly, then refrigerate
  8. Pour over ice and enjoy

Raspberry Vodka Lemonade

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of Vodka
  • 1 ½ cups fresh raspberries
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup lemon juice
  • 5 cups water

Instructions

  1. Bring 1 cup of water and the sugar to a boil, reduce heat and simmer until sugar has dissolved
  2. Let cool slightly, then pour into blender along with the raspberries
  3. Strain into a pitcher then refrigerate until cold
  4. After it has chilled, add the lemon juice and the remaining water and stir
  5. Pour 1 ounce of Vodka in each glass and top off with the raspberry mixture
  6. Garnish with raspberries
  7. Drink until refreshed
