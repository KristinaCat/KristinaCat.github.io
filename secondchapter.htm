Welcome To Our Bookshelf

We are not your ordinary second hand book store. We specialize in stocking the classics from your childhood, we cater to your requests for bringing in hard to find books from all over the world. Our stock is ever changing, we live for the hunt and sharing our great finds with those who appreciate the oldies.

We are proud to be a family run business, and pride ourselves on keeping the costs down for our customers. We have flat rate shipping of $1.00 to anywhere in Canada, No matter how many books you buy you can count on $1.00 shipping. We keep our costs down by keeping it simple - taking our own photos, maintaining our own website and networking with new leads. It's what we love to do!

Do you have a literary treasure on your mind? Contact us! We want to talk to you, hear your stories, and bring them back into your life!
