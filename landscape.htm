Don't move, Improve!

Extending your living space outside not only has the illusion of increased square footage, but studies have shown that spending more time out doors increases longevity and quality of life.

Lawn Enforcement was a small business venture started by Sir Bilbo Baggins in Kraken, British Columbia. Sir Bilbo was proud to serve in the strata of his local gated community, Cheshire Square, enforcing the strict lawn height rules of the community. 200 employees and 3 provinces later, we have expanded to a full service landscape architecture design and maintenance business specializing in custom water features and unique masonry work.

We are here to make your ideas work in any space no matter the size or terrain. Browse our gallery of previous projects to get a feel for what we can offer, and remember that what may seem impossible to do may not be so far fetched after all.

We can help you add years to your life and life to your years. Contact us for a free in home consultation.
