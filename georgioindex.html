Musician

If you have listened to the radio in the last year, chances are you have heard the melodies of James Georgio without even knowing it.
His love for the guitar started at eight years old, by ten he mastered the instrument and was performing all over Canada. But he didn't stop there. James was determined to expand his mind and dedicate himself to more than just the guitar. He wanted more, and so did his fans!

Composer

James started writing music at twelve years old. It didn't take long for his lyrics and melodies to catch the attention of some big names, one of which took him under their wings.
Carlos Santana. He was welcomed with open arms, and ears, into the world of music composition. James and Carlos became a pair who could not be stopped. They were a hit with all walks of life, travelling worldwide to share their musical talents.

Legend

From the little town of Timbucktoo Saskatchewan, James Georgio is a treasure and a local legend. He may be known all over the world these days, but he sure has stayed true to his roots. Still calls the house he grew up to home, makes sure to attend all family get-togethers, and even still produces music from his mother's basement.
James Georgio. Small town boy with big city accomplishments.
