Lemon Meringue Pie Recipes

MMmmmmmm....Lemon Meringue Pie...with ginger?!

Filling

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 1-1/2 cups water
  • 1-1/2 cups lemon juice
  • 1 tbs lemon zest
  • 2 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs grated ginger

Pastry

  • 1-1/4 plain flour
  • 1 tbs sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 6 tbs butter, cut into pieces and frozen
  • 4 tbs vegetable shortening, frozen
  • 3-5 tbs ice water, as needed
  • 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs

Instructions

  • Preheat the oven to 375F
  • Make crust
  • Mix filling ingredients and place in crust
  • Make and add meringue if desired
  • Bake for 20-25min
