Saturn: Gas Giant With Many Moons

Planet Facts

  • Made up of 96% hydrogen, 3% helium and 1% various other chemicals including ammonia and methane.
  • Second largest planet in this solar system.
  • The planet has such a low density that it would float in water.
  • In place of a solid surface, Saturn has layers of clouds.
  • 62 known moons orbit Saturn.
  • A year on Saturn is equal to 29.5 Earth years.
  • Saturn's rings contain billions of particles of water ice, and some rock materials.
  • Wind speeds can reach over 1,100 mph, and the lightning is 1,000 times more powerful than on Earth
  • Temperature on the "surface" is on average -168 degrees C.
