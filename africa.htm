Welcome to Africa!...Almost Africa.

Have you ever wondered what it would be like to go on an African safari and see exotic animals in their natural habitat? It is closer than you think!

Located just outside the city of Armstrong, hidden in a 2500 ft2 underground bunker, lies an oasis for humans and African native animals alike. The bunker has been turned into a Serengeti ecosystem; climate, landscape and even the timezone have been duplicated to create the ideal environment for those who call it home. We also have a fully equipped medical laboratory filled with the latest technologies to get these beautiful animals healthy again.

We currently are home to 18 animals which include; Gabby the giraffe, Leo the leopard and Barcode the zebra. The SPCA have entrusted us to rehabilitate and care for the exotic animals they seize from unsuitable properties across the province. We are here to give them back the life that they deserve, and we would love to show you around!

We have many guestrooms available all year round as well as plenty of activities and tours to keep you busy. Please contact us for full pricing and availability!
