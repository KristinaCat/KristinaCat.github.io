Cherry Pie Recipes

MMmmmmmm....Cherry Pie...with cheese?!

Filling

  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 4 cups fresh tart cherries, pitted
  • 1-1/2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 cup cream cheese

Pastry

  • 255g/9oz plain flour
  • pinch of salt
  • 140g/5oz hard margarine or butter
  • 6 tsp cold water

Instructions

  • Preheat the oven to 400F
  • Sieve the flour and salt into a bowl
  • Add the margarine or butter and mix until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs
  • Add the cold water to the flour mixture. Using your hand to firm up the mixture to good rolling consistency
  • Divide the pastry into two halves. Take one half and roll it out so that it is big enough to cover pie plate
  • In a large saucepan, combine the sugar, cornstarch and salt. Add the cherries, cream cheese, water and lemon juice. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened
  • Pour cherry mixture onto pie crust
  • Roll out the other half of the pastry. Moisten the edge of the bottom layer of pastry and place the second piece on top
  • Use fork to seal the edges of pie. Trim excess pastry
  • Prick the center of the pastry before placing the pie in the oven, and cover edges of pastry with foil
  • Bake at 400 for 15 minutes. Remove foil; bake 15-20 minutes longer or until crust is golden and filling is bubbly
