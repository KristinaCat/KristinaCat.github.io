Apple Pie Recipes

MMmmmmmm....Apple Pie....with pork?!

Filling

  • 3 large, tart apples, chopped, stewed and cooled
  • 1 small, cooked to your liking, pork roast
  • 8 tbs sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 4 tbs margarine or butter

Pastry

  • 255g/9oz plain flour
  • pinch of salt
  • 140g/5oz hard margarine or butter
  • 6 tsp cold water

Instructions

  • Preheat the oven to 400F
  • Sieve the flour and salt into a bowl
  • Add the margarine or butter and mix until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs
  • Add the cold water to the flour mixture. Using your hand to firm up the mixture to good rolling consistency
  • Divide the pastry into two halves. Take one half and roll it out so that it is big enough to cover pie plate
  • Cover the pastry with the stewed apples and layer with shredded pork roast
  • Sprinkle with sugar, cinnamon and dollops of the margarine or butter
  • Roll out the other half of the pastry. Moisten the edge of the bottom layer of pastry and place the second piece on top
  • Use fork to seal the edges of pie. Trim excess pastry
  • Prick the center of the pastry before placing the pie in the oven. Cook for 20-30 minutes
