Kleo’s Easy Casserole Pierogi Recipe

Miss Kleo is excited to share her go-to weekly plan of quick dinner recipes with you. Opposable thumbs not needed to recreate these mouth-watering easy casseroles for the favorite humans in your life.

It didn't take long for Kleo to realize that Monday seems to be her human’s least favorite day of the week, which makes it the perfect day to serve her signature easy casserole dish. This easy casserole pierogi recipe is the perfect pick me up after the dreaded back to work Monday blues. Prepare for extra pets after this delightful meal!

Ingredients

  • 1kg bag frozen pierogis
  • 4 pre-cooked smokies cut into ½ cm thick rounds
  • 1 finely diced small onion (optional)
  • 2 cans Cream of Bacon soup (any cream soup will work)
  • 2 cups milk (can substitute with 1 ½ cups of water)

You will also need a 9” x 11” casserole dish, tinfoil, and cooking spray (recommended).

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F.
  2. (Recommended Step) Spray your large casserole dish with cooking spray, this will prevent sticking and will ensure this quick dinner recipe has a quick clean up too.
  3. Empty the bag of pierogis into the casserole dish.
  4. Sprinkle your diced onion over the pierogis.
  5. Layer the top of the casserole with the chopped up smokies.
  6. In a bowl, mix together both cans of Cream of Bacon soup with the milk (or water).
  7. Pour the soup mixture evenly over top of the other ingredients.
  8. Cover with tinfoil and bake for 1 hour.
  9. Remove the tinfoil for the last 10 minutes of cooking to brown the smokies on the top layer.
  10. Let sit for about 10 minutes after taking out of the oven before serving.

Some felines do not agree with the practice of doing such work. “My human feeds me, so why not feed my human? His opposable thumbs seem to have hindered his hunting abilities, so I need to step up and take care of him.” Kleo claims that “he would not survive without me.”

Quick dinner recipe ideas come to Kleo in her many naps throughout the day. Creating easy casseroles are her specialty. “Sometimes I improvise and use rodent meat, that’s the only way he seems to accept my gifts. What my human does not know will not hurt him.” Kleo lovingly proclaims.
