Pecan Pie Recipes

MMmmmmmm....Pecan Pie...with peaches?!

Filling

  • 1 cup Corn Syrup
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tbs melted butter
  • 1 tsp Pure Vanilla Extract
  • 1-1/2 cups pecans
  • 1 cup peeled, sliced peaches

Pastry

  • 4.5 oz plain flour
  • pinch of salt
  • 2.5oz hard margarine or butter
  • 3 tsp cold water

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 350F
  • Sieve the flour and salt into a bowl
  • Add the margarine or butter and mix until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs
  • Add the cold water to the flour mixture. Using your hand to firm up the mixture to good rolling consistency
  • Roll out pastry and place in pie plate
  • Mix corn syrup, eggs, sugar, butter and vanilla using a spoon then stir in pecans
  • Layer pecan mixture and fresh peaches in pie crust
  • Bake on center rack of oven for 60 to 70 minutes. Cool for 2 hours
